Welcome to D&G Hairdressing!

D&G Hairdressing welcomes you to enjoy and pamper yourself in our relaxing and friendly boutique salon.

We are Dundonald's premier Unisex Hairdressers and have a wide range of treatments and packages available for everybody's needs!

At D&G Hairdressing we reach up to a whole new level. Our philosophy: constantly evolving in various directions: classic, casual, funky and artistically but always bearing in mind that hairstyles must be tailored and personalized to suit you!

Because hair is an extension of your individuality and sense of style, we're dedicated to creating fashionable looks that go with your personality.

Relax with a cool drink or tea/coffee and we'll have a chat and get to know you (and your hair), before we even think about cutting it! For us, at D&G Hairdressing, stunning cuts always start with a personal style consultation!

This'll give our stylists an insight into who you are and the statement you want to make to the outside world. We’ll design, just for you, an outstanding haircut that gives you a exclusive look with confidence boost.

Unwind in a comfortable environment with pleasant music, D&G Hairdressing's professional stylists provide you with the highest standard and latest trends in cutting and colouring. 100% human hair extensions, ionic crystallizing permanent straightening, organic herbal colour, perming, upstyle for weddings or other formal events and a variety of special deals are also available.
